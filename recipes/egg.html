egg recipe

ingredients

  • onion
  • egg
  • salt
  • garlic paste
  • curry
  • rice

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them lightly with a fork.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour the beaten eggs into the skillet.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for a few seconds until they start to set around the edges.
  5. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing them from the edges towards the center.
  6. Continue to cook the eggs, stirring occasionally, until they are just set but still slightly runny.
  7. Season the eggs with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the eggs rest for a moment before serving.
  9. Garnish with chopped herbs if desired and serve immediately.
